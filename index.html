About

öde is a short infinite exploration "game" made in 8 days for PROCJAM 2015.

Controls

Forward, backward, strafe left and right : arrow keys, WASD, d-pad or first stick.
Look around : Mouse or second stick.
Jump : X or button 3.
Jetpack : C or RT button.
